abstract: 'A two-stage framework that combines causal–semantic log pruning with LLM-based reasoning for interpretable 5G system analysis. PRISM uses a retrieval-augmented pipeline to condense millions of log lines into query-aligned templates (up to 30% higher factual accuracy and ~66% lower compute time) and a hypothesis–verification reasoning algorithm integrated with a 3GPP-derived knowledge graph to diagnose issues such as buffer overflows, RACH failures, bad channels, and HARQ retransmissions. Validated on over-the-air srsRAN testbeds with a multi-scenario 5G log dataset spanning SDR and commercial RU deployments. Advised by Prof. Dinesh Bharadia (UC San Diego).'
